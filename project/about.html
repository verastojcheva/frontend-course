About this Dashboard

Purpose

A compact view of product health for a SaaS/app: engagement, activation, retention, conversion, sentiment, and revenue.

Who it’s for

  • PMs / Product Analysts — weekly reviews & prioritization.
  • Founders & Leads — quick status without BI tools.
  • Stakeholders — shared snapshot for discussion.

When to use

  • Weekly product reviews & roadmap updates.
  • Before sprint planning to pick focus areas.
  • After launches/experiments to check movement.

What’s shown

Product Usage

  • DAU — daily active users.
  • Stickiness (DAU/MAU) — habit strength.
  • Time to Value (TTV) — speed to first “aha”.
  • D1 Retention — next-day return rate.

Conversion

  • Activation Rate — % reaching the key action.
  • Free → Paid — upgrade rate.

Customer Sentiment

  • NPS — promoters minus detractors.
  • CSAT — 1–5 post-interaction rating.

Revenue & Growth

  • North Star Metric — core value action per user.
  • MRR — recurring revenue.
  • Churn — % customers lost per month.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I read the dashboard quickly?

Start with your current goal (e.g., activation), then scan that group’s deltas for direction before digging into absolute values.

What’s the difference between DAU and Stickiness?

DAU is total daily users; Stickiness is DAU divided by MAU — a sense of habit strength across the month.

How should I interpret free → paid conversion?

Track changes after pricing or plan clarity tests. Pair with activation to ensure you’re not trading adoption for revenue.

Does churn include trial cancellations?

In this demo, churn reflects paying customers. Trials are monitored separately under activation and conversion.
